Overview

Built for the limit

The Chiron Super Sport is Bugatti's answer to a single question: how fast can a production car actually go. Built on the same 8.0-litre quad-turbocharged W16 that made the Veyron legendary, the Super Sport extends the formula further — longer tail, reworked cooling, retuned aerodynamics — all in pursuit of one number.

Inside, the cabin balances handcrafted luxury with the discipline of a car designed to hold 300+ mph in a straight line without drama. Every panel gap, every stitch, is part of the same engineering brief as the drivetrain.

Overview

The hardcore 720S

LT stands for Longtail, McLaren's badge reserved for the most track-focused, weight-obsessed version of a given platform. Built from the 720S, the 765LT strips roughly 80 kg out and adds 45 more horsepower, with a lengthened rear body and fixed rear wing for serious downforce.

Production was famously capped at a limited run, making the 765LT one of the rarer modern McLarens on the road. Inside, even the carpet is gone in favor of exposed carbon, every gram accounted for.
